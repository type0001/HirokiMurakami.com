Things worth knowing

Le Nomade MTL · 401 boul. Saint-Laurent

The hotel. Aparthotel with full kitchens in the units — that's what makes the Saturday cook-in work. Two minutes to Champ-de-Mars metro. Checkout Monday around 11am; they'll hold bags.

Booked. Bar George, Friday 6:00pm, table for six. Still to book: Mon Lapin for Saturday — opens 30 days ahead, caps at six, sells out in minutes — and Arthur's at Le Cathcart for Monday breakfast.

Getting around. Single fare C$3.75, 24-hour pass about C$11.50, three days about C$22. Buy an OPUS card at any station.

Paying. Tip 15–20%. Tax of roughly 15% is added at the register, not shown on the menu. For a table of six, many places add 15–18% automatically — check before tipping twice.

Language. Open with bonjour and staff switch to English immediately.

Groceries. Old Montreal has none. The IGA at Complexe Desjardins, 150 Ste-Catherine O., is a 10–12 minute walk.

A table for six. Plenty of places cap online booking at four or six. For walk-in spots, arrive at opening or split into two threes.

Before locking anything in, reconfirm hours — Crew Collective's Thursday closure, museum days, the Bota Bota booking, and the Piknic Sunday dates.
